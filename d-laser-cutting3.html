laser cutting robot

For this assignment we will show the design of a robot with a focus on simplicity and ease of use. The robot is designed to be assembled with few parts, ensuring that it can be put together quickly and efficiently without glue. Additionally, was designed with space to place an Arduino board and other components, making it easy to customize and modify.

 

The most important features of this robot is that it can be fitted with a screen to display a face for this companion. This feature makes the robot more engaging and interactive, allowing to customize the face and emotions.

Material: 2.5mm thickness acrylic

Design

The robot was designed using Adobe Illustrator.

The size of the holes was made to be 2.3 mm wide, taking into account the kerf and matching the thickness of the material.

Setting up the file in LaserCAD software

The AI file was then uploaded to LaserCAD software, where the corresponding settings were arranged. The silhouettes and holes were cut at a speed of 20 and power of 70, while the decorative parts were engraved at a speed of 300 and power of 30.

Laser cutting output. post processing
During this stage, a lot of dust was generated, which must be properly cleaned. The film should also be removed to get a better and neat aesthetic.
Dust is cleaned with water.
After drying, the film should be removed carefully.
Finally, the parts were painted with an aerosol painting bottle of white color. It is recommended to use gloves and mask for security purposes.

arduino connection About Project :

We are developing a laser-cutting project using Arduino that revolves around an emotion-based smart home power consumption indicator. The concept involves creating a robot-like model that visually expresses emotions based on the current power consumption in a household. When the electricity consumption is high, the model displays an angry face to signify concern.

Conversely, when the power consumption is low, the model shows a happy face to indicate a positive state. Under normal conditions, the model displays a neutral or normal emotion. This interactive system serves as a visual representation of the home's power usage, providing a unique and engaging way to monitor and respond to energy consumption levels.
