introduction

The journey our team embarked upon to build our website.

 

It was a collaborative effort that involved meticulous planning, innovative design, and proficient coding. Our journey began with the initial stages of conceptualization and design. We recognized the importance of a visually appealing and user-friendly website, and thus turned to a powerful design tool called Figma. With its intuitive interface and robust features, we were able to translate our ideas into a digital blueprint. Figma enabled us to create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes, allowing for seamless collaboration and iteration within our team.

 

Once the design phase was complete, our focus shifted towards transforming our vision into a functional website. We leveraged the power of GitHub, a popular version control platform, to streamline the development process. GitHub not only facilitated efficient code management but also allowed for seamless collaboration among team members .

design

Step 1: Designing the Character "Novva" using Leonardo AI

Our team took into consideration designing a character as an important initial step in creating a unique and visually appealing website. In this case, our team used the Leonardo AI to design a character named "Novva," who serves as the mascot representing our team.

 

Novva: A High-Tech Concept

To align with our Design Engineering subject, our team decided to incorporate a high-tech concept into the visual design of Novva and the website. This choice reflects the technological focus of our team's work and creates a cohesive theme throughout the website.

The high-tech concept can be expressed through various design elements, such as futuristic aesthetics, sleek lines, vibrant colors, and modern typography. By incorporating these elements, Novva can visually convey the innovative and cutting-edge nature of our team's work.

 

Importance of a Mascot

Having a mascot like Novva can bring several benefits to your website. A mascot adds personality, relatability, and memorability to your brand. It helps create a connection with our audience and can serve as a recognizable symbol for your team.

Novva can be featured in various sections of the website, such as the homepage, about page, or even as a guide throughout the user journey. By consistently incorporating Novva into the website's design, we create a cohesive and engaging user experience.

Step 2: Designing the Landing Page
After designing the character Novva, the next step was to create the landing page for our website. The landing page consists of two sections: an introductory section featuring Novva and a second section showcasing a carousel with the members of our team. This step involved using Figma, a popular design tool, to create the layout and define the visual elements of the landing page.

Creating the Figma Frame

To begin, our team created a frame in Figma to serve as the foundation for the landing page design. The frame acts as a container for all the elements within the landing page and helps maintain consistency throughout the design process. By setting up a frame, you establish the dimensions and structure of the landing page.

Defining Color and Typography Styles

To ensure a cohesive and visually appealing design, our team defined the color and typography styles for the landing page. This step involved selecting a color palette that complements the high-tech concept of Novva and the overall website design. Each member of the team was assigned a representative color, which adds a personal touch and helps distinguish individual team members within the carousel section.

Similarly, typography styles were established to maintain consistency in font choices, sizes, and hierarchy across the landing page. This helps create a visually pleasing and harmonious layout.

Using the Figma Auto Layout Tool

To assemble the landing page, our team utilized the Figma Auto Layout tool. This tool allows us to easily arrange and position the different sections, including the introductory section with Novva and the team carousel section. By leveraging the frame tool, we can ensure that the layout remains intact and that all elements are properly aligned.
