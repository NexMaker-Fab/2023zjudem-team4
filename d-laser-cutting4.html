laser cutting A scaled house model for our final project
Open Source: https://3axis.co/laser-cut-cottage-dollhouse-with-furniture-kids-toy-set-ai-file/lopqkpz7/

Material: 2mm thickness plywood.

We chose plywood for its flexibility, aesthetic appearance, and ability to fulfill technical and functional purposes in a scale model of a house.

Design

The file comes from an open source so it is already designed and prepared in .eps format and therefore editable in Adobe Illustrator.

Once opened we found out the original design is unnecessarily large for our purposes and represents a waste of material. For these reasons, we scaled the file size.

When doing this operation the width of the assembly holes must also be corrected to the appropriate size of the 2mm thick material used in addition to the Kerf correction.

Setting up the file in LaserCAD software

The AI file was then uploaded to LaserCAD software, where the corresponding settings were arranged.

The silhouettes and holes were cut at a speed of 30 and power of 30.
